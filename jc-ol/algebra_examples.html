Section A
Evaluating Expressions
Example Question
Find the value of 3p + 7q when p = 2 and q = 4
1
Substitute the values in
3(2) + 7(4)
2
Multiply each term
= 6 + 28
3
Add them together
= 34
✅ Answer:  34
✖️
Section B
Multiply Out and Simplify
Example Question
Multiply out and simplify:
2(3a + 4) + 5a
1
Multiply out the bracket
2 × 3a + 2 × 4 + 5a
= 6a + 8 + 5a
2
Collect like terms (group the a's)
= 6a + 5a + 8
3
Simplify
= 11a + 8
✅ Answer:  11a + 8

Section C
Solving Equations
Example Question
Solve for y:
8y + 4 = 2y − 8
1
Move all y's to one side
8y − 2y = −8 − 4
2
Simplify both sides
6y = −12
3
Divide both sides by 6
y = −12 ÷ 6
✅ Answer:  y = −2
